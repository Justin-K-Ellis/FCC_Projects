David Hume, Philosopher

About Hume:

  • One of the most important philosophers of the Enlightenment.
  • Arguably the most important philosopher in the English language.
  • Articulated a powerful vision of empiricism, skepticism, and naturalism.
  • The underlying goal of his philosophy was to argue for a secular, humanistic alternative to Christianity.
